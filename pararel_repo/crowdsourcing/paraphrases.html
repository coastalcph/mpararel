In this task, you need to decide if a pair of phrases are equivalent.

Task Definition

  • Read the given sentences.
  • Is there a bi-directional entailment relation between the two sentences?
  • The sentences contain an entity. Its purpose is to help understand the context of the sentences.
  • Some of the sentences are phrases, and not entire sentences.
  • Ignore the tense of the sentences for this task (ignore pragmatical considerations).
  • In some cases, there could be some world knowledge mismatch (e.g. "The country Paris") - Mark these as not-entailing
